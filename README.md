# HR Data Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze employee demographics, attrition, salary, job satisfaction, and workforce performance.

The dashboard helps HR managers monitor employee trends, identify attrition patterns, and make data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The HR department needs an interactive dashboard to monitor employee distribution, attrition, salary trends, and employee satisfaction in order to improve retention and workforce planning.

---

## 📊 KPIs

- Total Employees
- Active Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age
- Average Monthly Income
- Average Years at Company
- Average Job Satisfaction

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Employees by Department
- Gender Distribution
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Age Group
- Average Salary by Department
- Work-Life Balance Analysis
- Average Years at Company

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Sales department has the highest attrition rate.
- Employees under 25 show the highest attrition.
- Research & Development has the largest workforce.
- Sales Representatives experience the highest attrition.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Improve retention strategies for the Sales department.
- Strengthen onboarding for younger employees.
- Review compensation and career growth opportunities.
- Improve employee engagement and work-life balance.
